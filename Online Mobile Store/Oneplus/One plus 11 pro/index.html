The OnePlus 11 Pro is a flagship smartphone that combines state-of-the-art technology with elegant design. It features a 6.7-inch Fluid AMOLED display with a QHD+ resolution and a 120Hz adaptive refresh rate, delivering vivid visuals and smooth performance. The device is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, ensuring lightning-fast speed and efficiency. Its advanced camera system, developed in collaboration with Hasselblad, includes a 50MP main sensor, a 48MP ultra-wide lens, and a 12MP telephoto lens, providing exceptional photo and video quality. The OnePlus 11 Pro supports 5G connectivity for super-fast internet speeds. It is equipped with a 5000mAh battery, supporting 100W wired and 50W wireless charging, ensuring rapid and convenient charging. The phone's design features a premium glass back with Gorilla Glass Victus for enhanced durability. Running on OxygenOS, based on Android 13, it offers a clean, intuitive, and customizable user experience. Available in a range of stylish colors, the OnePlus 11 Pro merges high performance with sophisticated aesthetics, making it a standout choice for tech enthusiasts.
